Tiger Peanut Butter Candy

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup vanilla or white chips
  • 1/4 cup vanilla or white chips
  • 2 tablespoons milk chocolate chips

Directions

  1. In a small microwave-safe bowl, melt vanilla chips and peanut butter; stir until smooth. Advertisement
  2. Pour into a greased 5-3/4x3-in. mini loaf pan. In another microwave-safe bowl, melt chocolate chips; stir until smooth. Drizzle over first layer. Swirl together using a toothpick. Advertisement
  3. Freeze until firm, 5-10 minutes; break into pieces.
